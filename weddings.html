A typical ceremony will include...

  • Prelude (20-30min.) - Harp music played as the guests arrive and are seated.
  • Seating of the Mothers - Music as the Mothers come down the aisle.
  • Processional - Music for the Bridesmaids, and for the Bride's entrance.
  • Ceremony Music - Music for a candle-lighting, a reading, or communion.
  • Recessional - At the end of the ceremony as the wedding party departs down the aisle.
  • Postlude - Music as guests are leaving and greeting the wedding party.
